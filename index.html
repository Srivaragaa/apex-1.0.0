Our History

Sri Varagaa Electricals is a customer driven control system engineering company with expertise in electrical engineering, telemetry, automation, control, PLC, SCADA and RTU. Energy management, networking for IT and OT, and functional safety frameworks. The company was fully registered in 2011. Owned and operated in INDIA, our head office is situated at Chennai, Thiruvallur, Tamilnadu.
